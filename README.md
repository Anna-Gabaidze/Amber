# Amber

> Amber — keep the spark alive!

A productivity & motivation web app built at the **Hack the Halls** hackathon. Amber is made for beginner learners who get tired, overwhelmed, or unmotivated — it reminds you that small progress is still progress, and that showing up for five minutes counts. Log one tiny win, one concept understood, one session, and the spark slowly grows.

## What it does

- Lets you log small wins and study sessions to build consistency
- Focuses on encouragement and kindness over a "grind mindset"
- Simple, friendly interface aimed at students just starting out
